Privacy Policy

How Big is Baby Today does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

The due date and any measurement you enter are stored only on your device and are never sent anywhere. The app has no analytics, no tracking, and no user accounts.

Optional tips are processed by Apple through the App Store; we never see your payment information.

The size figures shown are population averages for information and fun only — they are not medical advice.
